I posted on LinkedIn every weekday for a month.

Two things I learned contradict each other completely:

1) Posting daily is easier than posting once in a while

For me, posting daily beats vague goals like "post twice a week." With daily, there's no decision, it just has to happen.

It reminds me of a Gretchen Rubin quote: "What you do every day matters more than what you do once in a while."

2) Posting daily is too much

While the rhythm is helpful, a post a day also has disadvantages:

Let go of the daily rhythm, and I'll probably miss my goals.

Keep it, and I'm stuck with the downsides.
