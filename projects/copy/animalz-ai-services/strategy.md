## SOCA (Single Overriding Communications Avoidance)
"This is experimental — we're still figuring it out and it'll take a while to see results."

Never communicate uncertainty about ability to deliver. No hedging, no caveats about AI limitations, no vague timelines, no "it depends" framing. Counter with proof of speed: same-day first drafts, trending content in 30-60 minutes, iteration within hours.
